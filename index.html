Wington de Lima Vital

Home

Wington de Lima Vital recently earned a Ph.D. in Applied Mathematics from Universidade Estadual de Campinas (UNICAMP). He is currently a data scientist and professor at Fundação Amazônica de Amparo à Pesquisa e Desenvolvimento Tecnológico Desembargador Paulo dos Anjos Feitoza.

His research combines theoretical and applied aspects of neural networks with weights in hypercomplex and vector algebras, focusing on universal approximation theorems and quantitative analysis of approximation complexity. Results obtained in this line of work allow for a rigorous assessment of the expressiveness of such networks, providing a theoretical foundation that connects algebraic structures, approximation theory, and machine learning. These approaches may contribute to a broader and more grounded understanding of neural network models that operate beyond real numbers.
